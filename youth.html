The Crew 6th-12th grades

The CREW (6th - 12th grade) meets on Wednesday nights from 5:30 - 7:00PM for dinner and small groups. In addition, the CREW meets on Sunday's from 5:30 - 7:00 for snack supper, games, bible lesson and worship.

This summer the CREW participated in a TeamEffort Mission camp in Copperhill, TN where they served others through home repair projects. In addition the CREW participates in an annual Youth Week with other UMC youth groups right here in Jackson. The CREW is looking forward to their annual Winter retreat to Breakthru after the holidays.
